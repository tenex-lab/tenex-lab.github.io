Column Definitions

avg_daily_roi_pct
Primary ranking metric. Average daily return on capital actually deployed, after fees. Measures how efficiently the model uses the capital it commits to trades.
mean( daily_pnl / daily_notional ) x 100
positive_day_ratio
Fraction of trading days that ended with a net profit. A high ratio means the model wins consistently day-to-day, not just on a few lucky days.
profitable days / total eval days
win_rate
Fraction of individual trades that were profitable (net of fees). Different from positive_day_ratio — a day with many small wins and one big loss can be a negative day but still have a high win_rate.
winning trades / total trades
total_trades
Total number of trades executed during the evaluation period. Low trade counts make other metrics less statistically reliable. Minimum required: 15 trades.
max_drawdown_pct
Largest peak-to-trough decline in the equity curve during the eval period. Expressed as a percentage. Closer to 0% is better — it measures the worst losing streak the model experienced.
( trough_equity - peak_equity ) / peak_equity x 100
eval_days
Number of distinct trading days included in the backtest evaluation. More days = more reliable estimate of real performance. Minimum required: 5 days.
model_id / version
ote_long: ICT/Smart Money rule-based strategy. Detects swing pivots, enters on Fibonacci OTE zone (62–79%) retracement with momentum confirmation.

rf_tp: Random Forest classifier. Predicts probability of price hitting a take-profit target within H minutes. Enters when confidence ≥ threshold.
symbol
The ETF/stock ticker the model was evaluated on. Each model is tested independently on SPY, QQQ, IWM, SOXL, and TQQQ. The leaderboard shows the best (model × symbol) combination.
